Shaniwar Wada

A historical fortification in Pune, built in 1732, it was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire.

Aga Khan Palace

Built in 1892, it is a majestic building and served as a prison for Mahatma Gandhi during the Quit India Movement.

Sinhagad Fort

Located 35 km from Pune, Sinhagad Fort has a long history and is famous for the Battle of Sinhagad in 1670.

About Pune

Pune is a vibrant city in Maharashtra, known for its rich history, educational institutions, and growing tech industry.
